biography

josh cumbee is an artist, producer and songwriter with a studio based in los angeles, ca. cumbee is a full-stack, jack-of-all-trades creative committed to a bruce lee-derived 'be like water' approach to collaboration and inspiration, and has had the great fortune of collaborating with greats such as sabrina carpenter, olivia rodrigo, madonna, kygo, steve aoki, armin van buuren, girl's generation, nct, shinee, monsta x, chris lee, lil ghost, ebi, and many many more.

with ~300 releases, +5b streams and counting, number one records in countries ranging from italy to korea, and a hearty dose of imposter syndrome, cumbee's likely either in his studio in la or on a plane to somewhere in the world with spicy food and a microphone.

josh produces in fender studio pro, and regularly plays a mix of keys, vocals, guitar, bass, and occasionally terrible trumpet.
